## Pricing FAQ (Inline)

### Why HKD$88? That's cheaper than a tutor session.
Exactly. One hour of private tutoring in Hong Kong costs HKD$200-500. XueBaOS gives you unlimited AI-powered memory palaces, a personalized scheduler, and adaptive testing for HKD$88/month. The value math isn't complicated — we're betting on volume, not margin.

### What's the difference between Basic and Advanced FSRS?
Basic FSRS uses population-average parameters — it works well for most people. Advanced FSRS learns *your* specific forgetting curve. It analyzes how quickly *you* forget different types of content and adjusts intervals accordingly. For most students, Advanced FSRS reduces review load by an additional 15-20%.

### Can I switch between plans?
Yes. Upgrade anytime — your existing palaces and data carry over seamlessly. Downgrade and you'll keep access to your content but lose Pro features (unlimited palaces, AI images, Advanced FSRS, etc.).

### Do you offer student discounts?
The annual Pro plan (HKD$888/yr, ~HKD$74/mo) is already deeply discounted. If you're facing genuine financial hardship, email us at support@xuebaos.ai and we'll figure something out. We're not here to gatekeep cognitive tools.

### What payment methods do you accept?
Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), AlipayHK, WeChat Pay HK, FPS (轉數快), and Apple/Google in-app payments.
