### KEEP DOING: What Worked

**1. 3-Pass Annotation for Every Chapter**

Biology textbooks are dense with terminology, diagrams, and processes. The 3-Pass Method is tailor-made for this format. Pass 1 (red pen): mark every definition, every enzyme name, every "X leads to Y" relationship. Pass 2 (geometry): draw rectangles around process clusters (photosynthesis, respiration, nitrogen cycle), triangles at rate-limiting steps, arrows for feedback loops. Pass 3 (symbols): draw stick-figure symbols for each process — a leaf with CO₂ entering and O₂ leaving, a kidney with a filter, a DNA helix unzipping.

Speed target: 20-25 minutes per chapter. Biology has ~20 DSE chapters. You can complete your first 3-Pass on the entire syllabus in about 8-10 hours over a week. Then repeat. The repetition IS the review.

**2. 20-30 Minute Topic Sweeps**

Biology rewards breadth. The DSE paper tests a wide range of topics, and neglecting one can cost you an entire Section B question. My protocol: every study day, sweep one topic in 20-30 minutes. Monday: Cells. Tuesday: Enzymes. Wednesday: Photosynthesis. Rapid annotation + 5 recall questions. The goal is maintaining familiarity across all topics, not mastering one at the expense of others.

**3. Speed Thinking Drills**

Set a 5-minute timer. Pick a biology process (e.g., "Describe the process of photosynthesis from light absorption to glucose production"). Write continuously for 5 minutes without stopping. Don't edit. Don't check notes. Just write. This builds exam fluency — the ability to produce coherent, structured answers under time pressure. Do one drill daily in the month before exams.

### How to Use XueBaOS for Biology

**Palace Builder: Map Biochemical Pathways**

Biology is the ideal subject for memory palaces because it's full of ordered processes. Build one palace per major system:

- **Photosynthesis Palace:** Your home. Front door = light absorption by chlorophyll. Living room = light-dependent reactions (photolysis, electron transport). Kitchen = Calvin cycle (carbon fixation, reduction, regeneration). Bedroom = factors affecting photosynthesis.

- **Respiration Palace:** Your school. Gate = glycolysis. Playground = link reaction. Classroom = Krebs cycle. Hallway = electron transport chain and oxidative phosphorylation.

- **Human Physiology Palaces:** One per system. Digestion in your kitchen. Circulation along your MTR commute. Excretion in a shopping mall you know well.

During Recall Arena in Palace Mode, mentally walk through each palace and dump the retrieved process onto a blank canvas. XueBaOS compares your output against the topic knowledge graph and highlights gaps.

**Symbol Forge: Master Diagrams**

Biology diagrams are worth marks — sometimes the diagram alone is 3-4 marks in a structured question. XueBaOS's Symbol Forge helps you practice drawing and labeling key diagrams under time pressure: the heart (chambers, valves, vessels), the nephron, the eye, the reproductive systems, DNA structure, mitosis and meiosis stages.

Set "Speed Draw Mode" to 2 minutes per diagram. Practice until you can produce a labeled, accurate diagram in under 2 minutes. In the exam, this buys you time for written answers.

**Recall Arena: Process-Specific Decks**

Build separate flashcard decks for:
- Definitions (200+ terms — photolysis, transpiration, osmoregulation, etc.)
- Processes (step-by-step: "What are the 4 stages of mitosis? Describe each.")
- Comparisons (mitosis vs. meiosis, arteries vs. veins, CNS vs. PNS)
- Diagrams (image occlusion on key structures)

Use FSRS scheduling. Biology flashcards have the highest ROI of any subject because the volume of terminology is enormous. You cannot "understand your way around" memorizing the name of every enzyme in the Krebs cycle. You just have to memorize it. Flashcards do that.

### 繼續做：咩有效

**1. 每個章節都用三層註釋**

生物教科書充滿術語、圖表、同過程。三層過濾法係為呢種格式度身訂造嘅。第一層（紅筆）：標記每一個定義、每一個酶名、每一個「X導致Y」嘅關係。第二層（幾何）：圍住過程集群畫長方形（光合作用、呼吸作用、氮循環），喺速率限制步驟畫三角形，用箭嘴標示反饋迴路。第三層（符號）：為每個過程畫火柴人級別符號——一塊有CO₂入同O₂出嘅樹葉、一個有過濾器嘅腎、一條解開緊嘅DNA螺旋。

速度目標：每章20-25分鐘。生物有大約20個DSE章節。你可以喺一星期內用大約8-10個鐘完成整個課程嘅第一次三層註釋。然後重複。重複就係複習。

**2. 20-30分鐘課題速掃**

生物獎勵廣度。DSE試卷測試廣泛嘅課題範圍，忽略一個可以令你失咗成條B部分題目。我嘅協議：每個溫書日，用20-30分鐘速掃一個課題。星期一：細胞。星期二：酶。星期三：光合作用。快速註釋＋5條回憶題。目標係維持所有課題嘅熟悉度，而唔係以犧牲其他課題為代價精通一個。

**3. 速度思考操練**

### 继续做：什么有效

**1. 每个章节都用三层注释**

生物教科书充满术语、图表、和过程。三层过滤法是为这种格式度身订造的。第一层（红笔）：标记每一个定义、每一个酶名、每一个「X导致Y」的关系。第二层（几何）：围住过程集群画长方形（光合作用、呼吸作用、氮循环），在速率限制步骤画三角形，用箭头标示反馈回路。第三层（符号）：为每个过程画火柴人级别符号——一块有CO₂进和O₂出的树叶、一个有过滤器的肾、一条解开的DNA螺旋。

速度目标：每章20-25分钟。生物有大约20个DSE章节。你可以在一星期内用大约8-10个小时完成整个课程的第一次三层注释。然后重复。重复就是复习。

**2. 20-30分钟课题速扫**

生物奖励广度。DSE试卷测试广泛的课题范围，忽略一个可以让你失了整条B部分题目。我的协议：每个温习日，用20-30分钟速扫一个课题。星期一：细胞。星期二：酶。星期三：光合作用。快速注释＋5条回忆题。目标是维持所有课题的熟悉度，而不是以牺牲其他课题为代价精通一个。

**3. 速度思考操练**

设定5分钟计时器。选一个生物过程（例如「由光吸收到葡萄糖生产描述光合作用的过程」）。不停地连续写5分钟。不要编辑。不要查笔记。只是写。这建立考试流畅度——在时间压力下产生连贯、有结构答案的能力。考试前一个月每天做一次操练。
