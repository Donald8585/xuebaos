# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** May 2026

## 1. Introduction

TranceLab ("we," "us," "our") operates the XueBaOS platform ("the Platform"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal data when you use our Services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the Hong Kong Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (PDPO) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1. Information You Provide

**Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password. If you register via a third-party service (Google, Apple), we receive your name and email from that service.

**Profile Information:** You may optionally provide a profile picture, academic level, target examinations, subjects of interest, and study goals.

**Payment Information:** If you subscribe to a paid tier, our payment processor collects your payment card details, billing address, and transaction information. We do not store full payment card numbers on our servers.

**User Content:** We collect the study materials, flashcards, annotations, memory palaces, practice answers, essays, and other content you create or upload to the Platform.

**Communications:** When you contact our support team or participate in surveys, we collect the content of your communications.

### 2.2. Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data:** We collect information about how you interact with the Platform, including features used, session duration, pages visited, study streaks, flashcard performance, and recall rates.

**Device Information:** We collect information about the device you use to access the Platform, including device type, operating system, browser type, IP address, and mobile network information.

**Log Data:** Our servers automatically record information including IP address, access times, pages viewed, and referring URLs.

**Cookies and Similar Technologies:** We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the Platform, remember your preferences, analyze usage, and personalize content. See our Cookie Policy for details.

### 2.3. AI Processing Data

When you use AI-powered features (e.g., Story Generator, QBank generation, content analysis), we process the content you submit to generate responses. This data is used to provide the service and improve our AI models, subject to the limitations described in Section 5.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

**Service Delivery:** To provide, maintain, and improve the Platform, including creating your account, delivering study content, generating AI responses, and tracking your learning progress.

**Personalization:** To tailor study recommendations, difficulty levels, review schedules, and content to your individual learning patterns and goals.

**Communication:** To send you account-related notifications, service updates, security alerts, and support responses. With your consent, we may send marketing communications about new features or offers.

**Analytics and Improvement:** To analyze usage patterns, diagnose technical issues, optimize performance, and develop new features. We use aggregated and de-identified data where possible.

**Legal Compliance:** To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes, and to protect our rights, privacy, safety, and property.

**AI Model Training:** To improve our AI models using anonymized and aggregated data. We do not use your personal User Content to train AI models without your explicit consent.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK, our legal bases for processing personal data include:

- **Contractual Necessity:** Processing necessary to provide the Services you've requested (e.g., creating your account, delivering study features)
- **Legitimate Interests:** Processing for our legitimate business interests, such as improving the Platform, preventing fraud, and analyzing usage (where such interests are not overridden by your data protection rights)
- **Consent:** Processing based on your explicit consent (e.g., for marketing communications, which you may withdraw at any time)
- **Legal Obligation:** Processing required to comply with applicable laws

## 5. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only as follows:

**Service Providers:** We share data with third-party vendors who help us operate the Platform, including cloud hosting (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud), payment processing, email delivery, and analytics services. These providers are contractually bound to use your data only as directed by us and in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

**AI Service Providers:** We use third-party AI providers to power certain features. Data you submit for AI processing is transmitted to these providers solely for generating responses and is not used by them for their own model training unless separately agreed.

**Legal Requirements:** We may disclose data if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of XueBaOS, our users, or the public.

**Business Transfers:** If TranceLab is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

**With Your Consent:** We may share data for other purposes with your explicit consent.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services. Specific retention periods:

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account exists, plus 90 days after account deletion for recovery purposes
- **Study Data and Progress:** Retained while your account exists. Anonymized study data may be retained for analytics after account deletion.
- **Payment Records:** Retained for 7 years as required by Hong Kong tax and accounting regulations
- **Communications:** Retained for 2 years for quality and dispute resolution purposes

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us or using the account deletion function in the Platform settings.

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1. GDPR Rights (EEA and UK Users)

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restriction:** Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Objection:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing
- **Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent at any time for processing based on consent

### 7.2. PDPO Rights (Hong Kong Users)

Under the Hong Kong Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance, you have the right to:
- Request access to your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate personal data
- Be informed of our policies and practices regarding personal data
- Request a copy of our data processing practices

### 7.3. Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at fiverrkroft@gmail.com. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law (typically 30 days for GDPR, 40 days for PDPO). We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

## 8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:

- Encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Access controls and authentication mechanisms
- Employee training on data protection
- Incident response procedures

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your country of residence, including Hong Kong, the United States, and other locations where our service providers operate. When we transfer data internationally, we implement appropriate safeguards, including:

- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
- Adequacy decisions where applicable
- Contractual obligations on service providers to maintain equivalent data protection standards

## 10. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Users between 13 and 18 must have parental or guardian consent. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13 without verified parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or through the Platform at least 30 days before taking effect. Your continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 12. Data Protection Officer

For questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, or to contact our Data Protection Officer:

**Email:** fiverrkroft@gmail.com
**Address:** TranceLab, Hong Kong SAR

## 13. Complaints

If you believe your data protection rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with:

- **Hong Kong:** Office of the Privacy Commissioner for Personal Data (www.pcpd.org.hk)
- **EEA:** Your local data protection supervisory authority

### 1. 引言

TranceLab（「我們」）營運 XueBaOS 平台（「平台」）。本私隱政策解釋我們如何在您使用服務時收集、使用、披露及保護您的個人資料。我們致力保護您的私隱並遵守適用數據保護法律，包括香港《個人資料（私隱）條例》（PDPO）及歐盟《通用數據保障條例》（GDPR）。

### 2. 我們收集的資料

#### 2.1. 您提供的資料

**帳戶資料：** 建立帳戶時，我們收集您的姓名、電郵地址及密碼。如通過第三方服務（Google、Apple）註冊，我們從該服務接收您的姓名及電郵。

**個人檔案資料：** 您可選擇提供個人頭像、學術水平、目標考試、感興趣科目及學習目標。

**付款資料：** 如訂閱付費層級，我們的付款處理商收集您的付款卡詳情、帳單地址及交易資料。我們不會在伺服器上儲存完整付款卡號碼。

**用戶內容：** 我們收集您在平台上創建或上載的學習材料、記憶卡、註釋、記憶宮殿、練習答案、文章及其他內容。

**通訊：** 當您聯絡我們支援團隊或參與調查時，我們收集您的通訊內容。

#### 2.2. 自動收集的資料

**使用數據：** 我們收集有關您如何與平台互動的資料，包括使用的功能、環節時長、瀏覽頁面、學習連續日數、記憶卡表現及回憶率。

**裝置資料：** 我們收集有關您用於存取平台的裝置資料，包括裝置類型、作業系統、瀏覽器類型、IP位址及流動網絡資料。

**記錄數據：** 我們的伺服器自動記錄包括IP位址、存取時間、瀏覽頁面及轉介URL等資料。

**Cookies及類似技術：** 我們使用cookies及類似技術來操作平台、記住您的偏好、分析使用情況及個人化內容。詳情請參閱我們的Cookie政策。

#### 2.3. AI處理數據

當您使用AI驅動功能（如故事產生器、QBank生成、內容分析）時，我們處理您提交的內容以生成回應。此數據用於提供服務及改善我們的AI模型，受限於第5條所述的限制。

### 3. 我們如何使用您的數據

我們將您的個人資料用於以下目的：

**服務提供：** 提供、維持及改善平台，包括建立您的帳戶、提供學習內容、生成AI回應及追蹤您的學習進度。

**個人化：** 根據您的個人學習模式及目標，度身訂造學習建議、難度水平、複習排程及內容。

**通訊：** 向您發送帳戶相關通知、服務更新、安全警報及支援回應。經您同意，我們可能發送有關新功能或優惠的市場推廣通訊。

**分析及改善：** 分析使用模式、診斷技術問題、優化表現及開發新功能。我們盡可能使用匯總及去識別化的數據。

**法律合規：** 遵守適用法律、法規及法律程序，並保護我們的權利、私隱、安全及財產。

**AI模型訓練：** 使用匿名化及匯總數據改善我們的AI模型。未經您明確同意，我們不會使用您的個人用戶內容訓練AI模型。

### 4. 處理的法律基礎（GDPR）

對於歐洲經濟區（EEA）及英國的用戶，我們處理個人資料的法律基礎包括：

- **合約必要：** 為提供您所請求服務的必要處理（如建立帳戶、提供學習功能）

- **同意：** 基於您明確同意的處理（如市場推廣通訊，您可隨時撤回）
- **法律義務：** 遵守適用法律所需的處理

我們不出售您的個人資料。我們僅按以下方式分享數據：

**服務供應商：** 我們與協助營運平台的第三方供應商分享數據，包括雲端託管（如AWS、Google Cloud）、付款處理、電郵發送及分析服務。此等供應商受合約約束，僅按我們的指示使用您的數據，並遵守適用數據保護法律。

**法律要求：** 如法律、法規、法律程序或政府要求所需，或為保護XueBaOS、我們的用戶或公眾的權利、財產或安全，我們可能披露數據。

**經您同意：** 經您明確同意，我們可能為其他目的分享數據。

### 6. 數據保留

我們在您的帳戶活躍期間或為提供服務所需的期間保留您的個人資料。具體保留期：

- **帳戶數據：** 帳戶存在期間保留，另加帳戶刪除後90天用於恢復目的
- **學習數據及進度：** 帳戶存在期間保留。匿名化學習數據可能在帳戶刪除後保留用於分析

- **通訊：** 保留2年用於質素及爭議解決目的

您可隨時聯絡我們或使用平台設定中的帳戶刪除功能要求刪除您的數據。

- 獲悉我們有關個人資料的政策及慣例
