# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** May 2026

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website or use an application. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and provide information to site owners. Cookies may be "session cookies" (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (remain until they expire or you delete them).

In this policy, "cookies" also refers to similar technologies such as web beacons, pixels, local storage, and device identifiers.

## 2. How XueBaOS Uses Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

### 2.1. Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are necessary for the Platform to function and cannot be disabled. They enable core functionality such as:

- User authentication and account security
- Session management (keeping you logged in)
- Load balancing and server routing
- Form submission and data storage
- Security features (CSRF protection, rate limiting)

**Examples:**
- `session_id` — maintains your login session
- `csrf_token` — protects against cross-site request forgery
- `auth_state` — tracks authentication status

### 2.2. Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages. If you disable these cookies, some features may not work properly.

**Purposes:**
- Remember your preferences (language, theme, accessibility settings)
- Remember your study preferences (active subjects, difficulty level, review intervals)
- Enable live chat and support features
- Store UI state (sidebar collapsed, last viewed page)

**Examples:**
- `pref_language` — stores your preferred interface language
- `pref_theme` — stores light/dark mode preference
- `study_defaults` — remembers your default subjects and settings

### 2.3. Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with the Platform by collecting and reporting information anonymously. We use this data to improve the Platform and your learning experience.

**Purposes:**
- Count visits and traffic sources
- Measure study session duration and feature usage
- Identify which pages and features are most/least used
- Track error rates and technical performance
- Understand user journeys through the Platform

**Examples:**
- `_ga` / `_ga_*` — Google Analytics (anonymized IP)
- `analytics_session` — session-level usage tracking

### 2.4. Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use the Platform to help us optimize performance.

**Purposes:**
- Measure page load times
- Track API response times
- Monitor error rates
- Optimize content delivery

## 4. Cookie Duration

- **Session Cookies:** Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent Cookies:** Remain for a set period:
  - Essential cookies: Up to 30 days
  - Functional cookies: Up to 1 year
  - Analytics cookies: Up to 2 years
  - Performance cookies: Up to 24 hours

## 5. Managing Cookies

### 5.1. Cookie Consent

When you first visit the Platform, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can:

- **Accept All:** Enable all cookie categories
- **Reject Non-Essential:** Enable only essential cookies
- **Customize:** Choose which categories to enable

You can change your preferences at any time through the "Cookie Settings" link in the Platform footer or settings menu.

### 5.2. Browser Controls

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through browser settings. You can:

- Delete cookies already stored on your device
- Block cookies from being set
- Set preferences for specific websites
- Browse in "private" or "incognito" mode

Browser-specific instructions:
- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox:** Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
- **Edge:** Settings → Site permissions → Cookies and site data

**Important:** Disabling essential cookies will prevent the Platform from functioning properly. You may not be able to log in or use core features.

### 5.3. Mobile Device Controls

On mobile devices, you can control tracking through:

- **iOS:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
- **Android:** Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

## 6. Cookies We DO NOT Use

XueBaOS does not use:

- **Advertising cookies** — we do not display third-party advertisements or use cookies for ad targeting
- **Social media tracking cookies** — we do not embed social media tracking pixels
- **Browser fingerprinting** — we do not use techniques to identify you based on your device configuration

## 7. GDPR and Cookie Consent

Under the GDPR and ePrivacy Directive, we:

- Obtain your explicit consent before setting non-essential cookies
- Provide clear information about each cookie category
- Allow you to withdraw consent at any time
- Do not use cookie walls (requiring cookie acceptance to access content)
- Record and maintain records of consent

## 8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our cookie practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will notify you through the Platform and update the "Last Updated" date.

### 1. 什麼是Cookies？

Cookies是當您瀏覽網站或使用應用程式時放置在您裝置上的小型文字檔案。它們廣泛用於使網站有效運作並向網站擁有者提供資訊。Cookies可能是「工作階段cookies」（關閉瀏覽器時刪除）或「持久cookies」（保留至到期或您刪除它們）。

### 2. XueBaOS如何使用Cookies

我們將cookies及類似技術用於以下目的：

#### 2.1. 必要Cookies（始終活躍）

這些cookies是平台運作所必需的，無法停用。它們啟用核心功能，例如：

- 用戶認證及帳戶安全

- 負載平衡及伺服器路由

**例子：**
- `session_id` — 維持您的登入工作階段
- `csrf_token` — 防止跨站請求偽造
- `auth_state` — 追蹤認證狀態

#### 2.2. 功能Cookies

這些cookies啟用增強功能及個人化。它們可能由我們或我們已添加到頁面的第三方供應商設置。如停用這些cookies，部分功能可能無法正常運作。

**例子：**
- `pref_language` — 儲存您的偏好介面語言

**目的：**

- 量度學習環節時長及功能使用
- 識別哪些頁面及功能使用最多/最少
- 追蹤錯誤率及技術表現
- 了解用戶在平台上的旅程

**例子：**
- `_ga` / `_ga_*` — Google Analytics（匿名化IP）
- `analytics_session` — 工作階段級別使用追蹤

#### 2.4. 表現Cookies

這些cookies收集有關您如何使用平台的資訊，以幫助我們優化表現。

**目的：**
